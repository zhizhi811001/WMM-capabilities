West Midlands Manufacturing Capability Survey

This form collects detailed capability data from local manufacturers for inclusion in the WMMITAN asset database. The database supports strategic matchmaking between manufacturers and technology startups, with an emphasis on advanced manufacturing and clean tech innovation in the West Midlands.

Please complete each section as accurately as possible. The form includes fields on production capabilities, quality systems, digital readiness, ESG commitments, and sector expertise. Your submission will help create a high-quality profile for use in regional innovation initiatives and supply chain development.

Note: Required fields are marked. Where applicable, please provide structured details (e.g., equipment specifications, certifications, project references).
